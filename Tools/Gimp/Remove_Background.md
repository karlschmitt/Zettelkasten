# 🎯 What to do next

## 1. Select the background

Use one of these tools:

* **Zauberstab** (_Fuzzy Select Tool_) → press **U**

* Click on the background

👉 Adjust **Schwellwert (Threshold)** if it doesn’t select everything

***

## 2. Delete it

* Press **Entf (Delete)**

👉 You should now see a **gray checkerboard pattern**\
\= **transparent background ✅**

***

# 🧼 If it looks messy (white edges etc.)

Quick fix:

* **Auswahl → Vergrößern** (1–2 px)

* Then press **Entf** again

Optional:

* **Auswahl → Weiche Kanten (Feather)**

***

# 💾 Don’t forget to export correctly

* **Datei → Exportieren als**

* Choose **PNG**

👉 JPG will destroy transparency ❌

***
