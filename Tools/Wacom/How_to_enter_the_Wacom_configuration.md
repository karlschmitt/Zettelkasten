## 🔌 Step 3: Connect Your Tablet

* Plug in your Wacom tablet via USB

After restart:

* Press **Windows key**

* Type: **Wacom Tablet Properties**

* Open it

* Pen pressure

* Button shortcuts

* Screen mapping

## 🧪 Step 6: Test It

Try:

## ⚠️ Common Problems (and Fixes)

### ❌ Tablet not recognized

* Try another USB port

* Use original cable

* Restart again

***

### ❌ “No device connected”

* Reinstall driver

* Make sure **Wacom service is running**:

  * Press `Windows + R`

  * Type: `services.msc`

  * Restart **Wacom Professional Service**

***

### ❌ Old driver conflicts

If you had issues before:

1. Uninstall old Wacom driver

2. Restart

3. Install fresh version

***

How to enter the wacom drawing pad configuration?

To open the **Wacom drawing tablet configuration (settings panel)** on Windows, you’ve got a few easy ways 👍

***

## 🧭 Method 1: Start Menu (Fastest)

1. Press **Windows key**

2. Type: **Wacom**

3. Click **“Wacom Tablet Properties”**

👉 This is the main control panel where you configure:

* Pen buttons

* Pressure

* Mapping

* Shortcuts

***

## ⚙️ Method 2: System Tray (Bottom Right)

Look near your clock (bottom right of screen):

1. Find the **Wacom icon**

2. Right-click it

3. Click **“Wacom Tablet Properties”**

👉 If you don’t see it, click the small **arrow (˄)** to show hidden icons

***

## 🔍 Method 3: Windows Search (Alternative)

1. Press **Windows + S**

2. Search for:

   * `Wacom Tablet Properties`

   * or just `Tablet`

***

## 📂 Method 4: Direct Path (Advanced)

If needed, you can run it manually:

* Press **Windows + R**

* Enter:

  ```
  C:\Program Files\Tablet\Wacom\Professional_CPL.exe
  ```

***

## 🧠 What You Should See

Once opened, you’ll get a window with sections like:

* **Pen**

* **Mapping**

* **ExpressKeys** (if your tablet has buttons)

👉 This is where you fix things like:

* Dragging issues

* Pen sensitivity

* Button assignments

***

## ⚠️ If You Can’t Find It

Then likely:

* Wacom driver is **not installed**

* or **not running**

👉 Fix:

* Install from: Wacom official website

* Restart your PC
